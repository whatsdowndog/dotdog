## what we help with

- **CI/CD** — pipeline design, build optimization, deployment automation, and getting your delivery cycle time down from "whenever Dave remembers" to continuous
- **Observability** — instrumentation, tracing, metrics, logging, and making sure you actually understand what your systems are doing before they stop doing it
- **Performance tuning** — profiling, load testing, bottleneck analysis, and the kind of deep-dive work that turns "it's slow" into "it's fixed"
- **Infrastructure migration** — moving between cloud providers, containerization, and all the terrifying cutovers that come with it
- **Incident management** — on-call design, runbooks, post-incident review, and building a culture where incidents make you stronger instead of just sadder
