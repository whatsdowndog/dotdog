Downdog is [**Isobel Redelmeier**](https://ca.linkedin.com/in/iredelmeier) and [**Spike Lindsey**](https://ca.linkedin.com/in/spikelindsey) — two SREs with 20+ years of combined experience, based in Toronto.

We've previously worked at Discord, Shopify, Lightstep, Wealthsimple, Pivotal, and Monzo. We started our careers as app developers and consultants at XP consultancies before moving into SRE, which means we tend to think about reliability from the perspective of the teams shipping code — not just the infrastructure running it.

We formed Downdog at the end of 2024 to do independent SRE consulting. After a year of intense client work, we scaled down our consulting engagements to focus on building product. Somehow, we missed having repositories and infrastructure of our own to tend.

We fall squarely into the "we have seen some wild shit" category of SREs, and are routinely paranoid about things most people have never considered being scared of. It's fun being us (kinda).
