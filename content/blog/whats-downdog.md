## No But Really, What's Downdog?

Downdog is a company we formed at the end of 2024 so that we could do some independent SRE consulting. After a year of intense client work, we decided to scale down our consulting engagements and focus more on product.

Somehow, we missed having repositories/infra of our own to tend, and wanted to see if we can put everything we've learned over our 20+ combined years of experience into practice. It's also allegedly easier to scale code/product than ourselves - we will report back on how true that is.

### Who Is "We"?

Downdog is (currently) [**Isobel Redelmeier**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/iredelmeier/) and [**Spike Lindsey**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/spikelindsey/). We've been SREs for most of our careers, with brief initial stints as app developers and consultants at well-known(ish) XP consultancies.

We fall squarely into the "we have seen some wild shit" category of SREs, and are routinely extremely paranoid about things most people have never considered being scared of. It's fun being us (kinda).

### Why Should I Subscribe?

Honestly, at this stage, we don't know! We started this newsletter to kick ourselves out of engineer-mode and figure out how to reach people outside of our friendly Toronto network. We also wanted a way to hold ourselves accountable to writing more - a weekly (ish) newsletter felt like a good way to do it.

We promise to artisanally hand-knit all of these e-mails, not use AI (...more on that in a future update), and actually be real people rather than an amorphous corporate blob. At least until we are wildly successful.
